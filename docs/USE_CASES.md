# Use Cases

## 1. Smart Agriculture

Monitor irrigation controllers, soil sensors, pumps, and valve systems remotely. Use AI to summarize field state, recommend actions, and trigger approved commands.

## 2. Industrial Equipment Monitoring

Track machine status, capture telemetry, push updates to remote controllers, and let operators issue controlled commands from a central dashboard.

## 3. Building Automation

Manage HVAC nodes, lighting controllers, occupancy systems, and energy monitors through one platform with policy-based automation.

## 4. Research and Education Labs

Coordinate fleets of embedded boards used in classrooms or labs, making provisioning, firmware rollout, and experiment control much easier.

## 5. Utility and Energy Systems

Use the platform for edge-connected meters, relay boards, or monitoring nodes where OTA reliability and remote diagnostics are critical.

## 6. Productized Hardware Platforms

If this becomes a deployable product, the combination of dashboard + OTA + AI assistant can differentiate the platform from basic device dashboards that only show status.
