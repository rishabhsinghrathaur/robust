# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims to be a respectful, technically serious, and collaborative environment.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate professionally
- review ideas on technical merit
- give actionable feedback
- avoid harassment, hostility, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- abusive language
- discriminatory behavior
- intimidation or harassment
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or contributions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior undermines a safe and productive collaboration environment.
